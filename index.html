About Kickbase

Germany's Most Trusted Fantasy Football Brand

Founded in 2012, Kickbase is the only officially licensed fantasy football platform for the German Bundesliga — with full rights across the 1st and 2nd division, DFB-Pokal, GPFBL, Baller League, LaLiga, and MLS. Thirteen seasons of consistent growth have established Kickbase as the category-defining product in a passionate, underserved market.

Growth Strategy

Two Clear Paths.
One Proven Playbook.

Kickbase's capital-efficient model and licensed product foundation create two high-conviction growth vectors: cracking the US market through MLS at the perfect cultural moment — and cementing an unassailable position in Germany.

🇺🇸
Primary International Target
USA · MLS Breakthrough

MLS rights are already secured — the commercial foundation is in place. The United States is in the middle of a soccer revolution: MLS viewership has doubled since 2020, youth participation outpaces every other team sport, and the cultural tipping point is imminent.

🏆
FIFA World Cup 2026 — The Perfect Launch Window
The USA, Canada & Mexico host the World Cup in summer 2026 — the largest sporting event on earth, landing in Kickbase's primary expansion market. An estimated 5M+ international visitors, ~3.5B TV viewers, and an unprecedented domestic surge in soccer engagement create a unique acquisition opportunity that won't repeat for a generation. Kickbase is positioned to launch ahead of the tournament and ride the wave.
